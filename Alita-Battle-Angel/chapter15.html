With her decreased mass, one arm was all she needed. She guided herself into a perfect landing on her open hand, then lowered herself slowly to the cement. Bright-blue cyber-blood was still pouring out of her, although it was starting to slow. She was in a pool of it now but she knew there was enought left for what she wanted to do.

格鲁依什卡艰难地站起来, 愤怒的咆吼. 天哪, 愤怒使他愚蠢. 正当他怒冲冲地朝她冲过来的时候, 阿丽塔将她的身体伸开, 展平她的的胳膊肘, 将自己推射到空中, 旋转着朝格鲁什卡尔奔过去. 她的手臂伸直, 手掌平齐聚成掌刀, 笔直地插到他的眼睛里. Grewishka struggled to his feet, roaring with rage. Good; anger made him stupid. As he came thundering at her, she raised her body up, flexed her elbow and launched herself into the air. Sending herself into a spin, she aimed herself at Grewishka. Keeping her arm straight out, she flattened her hand like a blade and plunged it into his eye.

现在他的叫声又尖又颤抖, 充满了疼痛和恐惧的感觉. 阿丽塔稳住身形, 直视他的另一只完好的眼睛. Now his scream was high and shrill, full of pain and terror. Alita smiled as she steadied herself so she could look directly into his other eye.

"去你妈的宽恕," 她说. "Fuck your mercy," she told him.

她尝试将她的手抽出来, 但是它在里面卡住了, 也许是被某个配件卡住了. 某种程度上, 她想, 这样说不定更好. 她扭动她的手, 前臂在手腕处断裂开来, 她从他身上掉下来. She tried to yank her hand free but it was caught on something inside, probably one of his enhancements. In a way, that was even better, she thought. She twisted hard until her forearm cracked and broke just above her wrist and she dropped away from him.

好好享受这个纪念品, 这可比吊坠好多了. 上面还刻着花呢. 我把一朵花留在了你的眼睛里面, 漂亮的花. 不客气! Enjoy the souvenir, It's better than a pendant. There's a flower on it. I left a flower in your eye, a beautiful flower. You're welcome.

格鲁依什卡的叫声现在多了一份狂怒, 但是并不是很多 — 他声音里更多的成分仍然是疼痛. 阿丽塔看着他笨拙地朝她走来, 用手盖着种了朵花了的眼睛. 这朵花会一直在那里的, 阿丽塔这样想; 即使人们从他的眼睛里把它拔出来, 给他装上一只新的更好的眼睛, 他也会一直记得她的这朵小花给他带来的疼痛的感觉, 只要他还活着. Grewishka's scream deepened with outrage, but not a lot — there was still more pain than anything else in his voice. Alita watched him lumber towards her, his hand over the eye with the flower in it. The flower would always be there, she thought; even after they took it out and gave him a new and better eye, he'd still feel the pain of her pretty little flower for as long as he lived.

阿丽塔朝着格鲁依什卡笑着, 而他正要抬起他的脚踩在阿丽塔身上. She smiled up at him as he raised his foot to stomp her.

她看到接下来的事情是格鲁依什卡卡向边上蹒跚了两步. 当她听到确信无疑的火箭锤的的声音的时候, 一阵喜悦涌上她的心头. 接下来一只燃烧瓶在格鲁依什卡身上炸裂开, 火焰吞噬了他的上半身, 又让他尖叫了起来. The next thing she knew, Grewishka was staggering sideways. Joy surged through her as she heard the unmistakable sound of the Rocket Hammer. In the next movement, a fire-bottle broke over Grewishka, engulfing his upper body in flames, making him scream again.

阿丽塔瞥见了雨果, 但在这之前, 有些迅速而凶猛的生物扑到了格鲁依什卡身上, 龇牙咧嘴, 低声怒吼, 将他赶开了. 其余的地狱犬正在攻击他, 撕开他的护甲, 撕裂内部的器件. Alita caught a glimpse of Hugo before something fast and vicious slammed into Grewishka, snarling and growling as it drove him back. Then the rest of hellhounds were on him, ripping his armour, going for his exposed innards, biting and snapping.

格鲁依什卡一边将地狱犬扯开, 一边尖叫, 逃离进了一条通道. 地狱犬仍然在追赶他, 凶狠地犬吠, 直到训犬大师出现, 召集回他的狗. "停下! 回来! 马上!" Still screaming, Grewishka tore the Hellhounds away from himself and fled into a passageway. The Hellhounds went after him, barking furiously uitl McTeague appeared and hollered, "Break off! Come! Now!"

地狱犬四肢奔腾, 跑回他的身边, 围成了一个保护性的包围圈, 包围了他, 依德和雨果. 阿丽塔记得她的朋友 — 那条无主的小狗, 眼泪顿时涌上了眼眶. 训狗大师和她目光相对, 眼里充满了同情. The Hellhounds came galloping back to him and formed a protective circle around him, Ido and Hugo. Alita remembered her friends, the little stray, and felt her eyes well with tears. McTeague met her gaze with eyes shiny with empathy.

"他不喜欢狗," 训狗大师严肃地说, "我可不喜欢这样." "He wasn't a dog-lover," McTeague explained grimly. "I hate that."

依德已经几乎听不见训狗大师的话了. 他跪在阿丽塔破碎的身体边上, 用手把她的头发从脸上往后梳. 阿丽塔抬头看着他; 他发现, 经历了这些风风雨雨, 他的心仍然会受伤. Ido barely heard McTeague's pronouncement. He fell to his knees beside Alita's torn and shattered form and brushed her hair back from her face. She looked up at him; and he discovered his heart could still break after all.

噢, 他的小女孩. 他曾经的女儿, 这样的事情又发生了. 一个恶魔, 因他而起, 不受控制, 四处为虐; 而他的女儿恰好挡在了路上. 绮莲事实上打造了这个怪物只是技术问题 — 他才是触发整个事件, 造成现在的局面的人, 他没有做任何事情来阻止这场厄运的变本加厉. His little girl. She was his little girl, and it had happended again. A demon he was responsible for had gone on a rampage and his little gir had been caught in its path. That Chiren had actually made this monster was only a technicality — he had set all the things in motion that had led to this, and he had done nothing to stop this horror from getting loose.

噢, 他的阿丽塔, 她并不是无助的; 她曾竭尽所能与这个邪恶的造物殊死一搏 — 真的是竭尽所能 — 她甚至给这个造物造成了严重的伤害并阻止了他. 但是这个恶魔太过强壮, 疯狂, 甚至邪恶. 即使是她对他造成了如此的重创, 她也从未真正有机会与他一决高下. 依德没能保护好她 — 即便是他本可以. His little Alita. She hadn't been helpless; she had fought the creature with everything she had — literally everything — and she had damaged it badly and driven it off. But the demon had been too strong, too crazed and too evil. For all that she'd hurt him, she'd never really had a chance against Grewishka. Ido had failed to protect her — even though he could have.

噢, 他的小姑娘. 本可以用来保护她的那件东西正躺在他的实验室的手术台上, 但是他拒绝为她装配这具身体. 相反的, 他在正义的自我坚持使得他像是一个道德傻瓜, 自我说服不给她狂战士的身体可以免于她再次成为人形武器, 这是最好的保护她远离邪恶的办法. His little girl. The very thing that could have protected her was lying on a table in his lab, but he'd refused to give it to her. Instead he'd been a self-righteous, moralising fool, convinced that not giving her the power of the Berserker body was the best way to protect her from evil.

也许这是对的 — 只不过不是在这个世界里, 在任何其他的世界里是有可能的. It might have been — in any other wolrd but this one.

噢, 他可爱的阿丽塔. 在历经种种一切之后, 他仍然拒绝相信这个世界的本质, 对环绕在她身边的危险视而不见. 更糟的是, 他一直拒绝她真实的身份, 企图改造她 — 重造她 — 变成一个甜美的, 漂亮的用花来装饰的年轻女孩. 结果他让她彻底失望了. His little Alita. After everything that had haappened, he'd managed to remain in denial about the nature of this world and the dangers around her. Worst of all, he'd been in denial as to who she really was, trying to make her — remake her — into a sweet, beautiful young girl adorned with flowers. As a result, he had failed her completely.

依德用自己的胳膊将她拢在怀里. Ido gathered her up in his arms.

他们走到街上, 依德让雨果走前门, 用火箭锤前后划拉, 为他和他怀里的珍贵的负担开道. 他保护性地将阿丽塔抱在怀里. 他感受到自己呼吸里面的深沉, 稀稀拉拉的抽噎. 此刻他不在乎任何人或者任何事, 除了将他的宝贝女儿带回实验室中. Once they were on the street, Ido had Hugo run ahead, sweeping the Rocket Hammer back and forth to clear the way from him and the precious burden in his arms. He held Alita to his chest protectively, feeling his breath coming in great, ragged sobs, not caring about anyone or anything except getting his little gir back to the lab.

绮莲站在堪萨斯酒吧撑开的门脸的阴影下. Then Chiren stepped out of shadows near the boarded-up Kansas Bar.

如果此刻雨果用火箭锤锤这个女人, 依德想, 他会假装没看见. If Hugo hit her with the Rocket Hammer, Ido thought, he would pretend not to notice.

"你以为一切会那么容易?" 绮莲冲他喊. "尽管治好她, 但是这样的事情我们可以一直做下去 — 如果有必要的话! 你能吗? 戴森? 你能吗?" "You think it'll be that easy?" Chiren called to him. "Bring her back all you want, but we can do this indefinitely — forever if we have to! Can you? Can you, Dyson?"

依德从她身边擦过, 对她视而不见. 他感受到她的目光, 想象她枯萎, 凋零, 向内崩塌. 因为她的内心已经空空如也, 一无所有. Ido pushed past her like she wasn't there. He felt her staring after him and imagined her wilting, shrivelling, collapsing in on herself because there was nothing insde, nothing at all.

依德一路小跑, 但是他并不是在逃离绮莲, 这一点她很清楚 — 她并不是自欺欺人. 现在他有一些东西要求他必须一路小跑. 很早之前她就失去了那样东西. — 对于他们来说都是失去 — 不会有任何事情改变这个事实. Ido was running but Chiren didn't try to kid herself — he wasn't running from her. Now he had something to run to. She'd lost that a long time ago — they both had — and nothing would ever change that.

但是他走了. 她留下来了. But there he went. And here she stayed.
